2. General terms of use of the site and services offered.

This site is a university work. Use of the site is strictly limited to the framework of pedagogical evaluation. Information regarding the festival (August 5-7 at Parc Monceau) originates from an exercise scenario.

3. Description of provided services.

The site simulates the presentation of the association's services, including:

  • Promotion of a film festival organized at Parc Monceau.
  • Promotion of a selection of independent films on the theme "The Journey".

The editorial content was produced for demonstration purposes to ensure an integration adapted to web constraints and natural search engine optimization (SEO).

No personal data is actually collected. The mentioned traffic analysis serves solely for learning audience measurement tools.
