✈️ Destination Monceau: Travel at the heart of our 5th edition

Theme | By Jennifer Vialet

For its fifth consecutive year, the "Open-Air Film Festival" is reaching a new milestone in its visual and artistic identity. While previous editions offered an eclectic selection, Jennifer VIALET and her association have chosen to take you on a unique thematic adventure this year: Travel.

         

An invitation to a motionless getaway

From August 5th to 7th, every evening from 6:00 PM to midnight, the lawn of Parc Monceau will no longer be just a Parisian garden, but a window open to the world. The choice of travel as a guiding thread is no coincidence: it echoes the very nature of auteur cinema, which invites us to leave our certainties behind to explore distant horizons, whether geographical or internal.

         

A carefully crafted program

In order to provide a high-quality filmographic resource, the team analyzed numerous works to select only the dozen films that will make up the final lineup. This rigorous selection aims to:

Sublimate auteur cinema: By highlighting directors who use the camera as a travel journal.

Harmonize the experience: So that each evening is a consistent stopover within a great cinematographic journey.

Reach a wide audience: By offering universal stories that speak of discovery and self-improvement.

A digital and local influence

Because the festival's reputation is growing, we are committed to sharing this passion with as many people as possible. Thanks to the support of our long-standing partners — the City of Paris, the Île-de-France Region, and France Télévisions — this journey will be shared across all digital platforms to raise awareness among future spectators about the magic of open-air screenings.

Our new website, featuring a polished graphic design, was designed to be your first travel guide even before the park gates open.

Ready for takeoff?

Whether you are a Sunday explorer or a seasoned film buff, pack your bag (don't forget your sweater!) and join us for these three exceptional nights at 35 Boulevard de Courcelles.

🎟️ The world is within sight, and the ticket is free.
