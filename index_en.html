About

Organized by the "Les Films de Plein Air" association, our festival celebrates auteur cinema every year in the heart of Parc Monceau in Paris. From August 5 to 7, 2026, from 6 PM to midnight, we invite the public to discover a unique selection of films screened under the stars. For this fifth edition, let yourself be transported by our central theme: The Journey.

Book now!

Featured Film

Distinguished by international critics, the film Arco has already won over many prestigious festivals thanks to its unique aesthetics and its initiatory journey narrative. The strength of its direction and the originality of its screenplay have allowed it to win several major awards, confirming this feature film's place as an essential work of contemporary auteur cinema. This critical success testifies to the exceptional artistic quality that Jennifer Vialet wishes to highlight for this edition of the festival.
